Casual mobile racing game, in the theme of [lawnmower racing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CtIYSeQd4gE).
My college thesis project, a rapid prototype commissioned from me by a game company.
<br><br>
I was responsible for basically everything in the project except the 2D UI sprites.
- Time-attack racing gameplay
- Touch & gyro controls
- Environment assets in Blender from scratch
- Rigged & animated player character
### Engine: Unity, Languages: C#
