Isometric 2D roguelike bullet-hell shooter developed for the Pelifarmi training program in a span of ~5 weeks.
<br>
[Link to the game page](https://samuel-salo.itch.io/boiro/)
<br><br>
In this project I was in charge of project & scrum management, and implementing features including:
- Bullet-hell projectiles
- Enemy AI & pathfinding
- Procedural room generation
- Modular weapons & pickups
- Isometric top-down shooter gameplay
### Engine: Unity, Languages: C#
