*You awake to your worst nightmare: you're at work, your hand is all kinds of messed up, and all of your coworkers are even more terrifying than before.*

A doom-inspired 2.5D survival shooter in an electronic body horror world. Prototype made for Pelifarmi training program in the span of ~5 weeks. <br>
[Link to the game page](https://samuel-salo.itch.io/clikr)
<br><br>
I implemented FPS game mechanics such as:
- Enemy AI & pathfinding
- FPS combat
- Pickups, interactables, dialogue
### Engine: Unity, Language: C#
