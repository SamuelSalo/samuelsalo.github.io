*Play as a scientist that travelled in time to the pre-historic era, but cannot travel back.*
3D sandbox survival adventure game developed for a university course.
[Link to the game page](https://darralord.itch.io/set-in-stone/)
<br><br>
In this project I worked on gameplay systems such as: 
- Player movement, combat & gathering
- Player equipment, inventory & stats
- Enemy AI & pathfinding
### Engine: Unreal Engine 5, Languages: Blueprints
