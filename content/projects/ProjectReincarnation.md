*You find yourself in a mysterious dungeon. Find your way out. Play as a human or monster, and switch sides upon death.*
2D isometric dungeon crawler RPG with roguelike elements, developed as a University course project.
[Link to the game page](https://samuel-salo.itch.io/project-reincarnation)
<br><br>
I was the sole programmer in this project, and implemented things such as:
- 2D isometric action combat
- Enemy AI & pathfinding
- Roguelike & RPG systems
- Modular roguelike perks
- RPG leveling system
### Engine: Unity, Languages: C#
